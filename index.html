Understanding AI

A beginner-friendly guide to Artificial Intelligence

🤖 What is AI?

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is the simulation of human intelligence in machines.

📊 What is Machine Learning?

ML is a subset of AI that enables machines to learn from data and improve over time.

🧠 What is Deep Learning?

Deep Learning is a specialized form of ML using neural networks to model complex patterns.

🌍 Real-life Applications

  • Speech recognition (e.g., Alexa, Siri)
  • Recommendation systems (e.g., Netflix, YouTube)
  • Self-driving cars
